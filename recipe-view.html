Fresh Vegetable Salad

15 mins Easy Vegetarian Serves 4

A refreshing mix of seasonal vegetables with a light vinaigrette dressing. Perfect for a healthy lunch or as a side dish for dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and prepare all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cucumber, bell pepper, carrot, red onion, and cherry tomatoes.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 1 hour before serving.

Nutrition Information

120 Calories
9g Fat
10g Carbs
2g Protein

Grilled Salmon with Herbs

25 mins Medium Seafood Serves 2

Perfectly grilled salmon fillet with fresh herbs and lemon. A protein-rich dish that's both healthy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat (about 375°F).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, dill, and parsley.
  3. Pat salmon fillets dry with paper towels and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Brush the herb mixture over the salmon fillets.
  5. Place salmon on the grill, skin side down, and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  6. Carefully flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes until the salmon is cooked through but still moist.
  7. Serve with lemon slices and additional fresh herbs if desired.

Nutrition Information

320 Calories
18g Fat
2g Carbs
36g Protein

Berry Smoothie Bowl

10 mins Easy Vegan Serves 1

A nutritious breakfast bowl with mixed berries, banana, and granola. Start your day with this antioxidant-rich, energizing meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup mixed frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Toppings: fresh berries, sliced banana, granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes

Instructions

  1. Add frozen banana, mixed berries, and almond milk to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. Add more almond milk if needed, but keep it thick.
  3. Add honey or maple syrup if desired and blend briefly.
  4. Pour into a bowl and arrange toppings artfully on top.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy with a spoon.

Nutrition Information

280 Calories
4g Fat
58g Carbs
5g Protein

Roasted Vegetable Pasta

35 mins Medium Vegetarian Serves 4

Whole grain pasta with oven-roasted seasonal vegetables and herbs. A hearty and satisfying meal that's full of flavor and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz whole grain pasta
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp Italian herbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. On a large baking sheet, toss zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomatoes, and garlic with olive oil, Italian herbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread vegetables in a single layer and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender and slightly caramelized.
  4. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the roasted vegetables and pasta. Add a splash of pasta water if needed to loosen.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese if using and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  7. Serve warm.

Nutrition Information

320 Calories
10g Fat
48g Carbs
12g Protein

Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

15 mins Easy Breakfast Serves 2

Sourdough toast topped with mashed avocado, poached egg, and chili flakes. A protein-packed breakfast that will keep you energized all morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough bread until golden and crisp.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer and add white vinegar.
  4. Crack each egg into a small cup, then gently slide into the simmering water.
  5. Poach eggs for 3-4 minutes until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
  6. Spread mashed avocado on toast slices.
  7. Top each toast with a poached egg, sprinkle with chili flakes, and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

Nutrition Information

350 Calories
22g Fat
28g Carbs
14g Protein

Quinoa Buddha Bowl

30 mins Medium Vegan Serves 2

Protein-packed quinoa bowl with roasted vegetables and tahini dressing. A balanced meal that's as beautiful as it is nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 sweet potato, diced
  • 1 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the dressing: 2 tbsp tahini, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp maple syrup, water to thin

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cook quinoa in water or broth according to package instructions.
  3. Toss sweet potato and chickpeas with olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  5. Steam broccoli florets until bright green and tender-crisp.
  6. Whisk together tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, and enough water to create a pourable consistency.
  7. Assemble bowls: quinoa on the bottom, arranged sections of roasted sweet potato, chickpeas, broccoli, avocado, and red cabbage.
  8. Drizzle with tahini dressing and serve.

Nutrition Information

450 Calories
18g Fat
60g Carbs
15g Protein
